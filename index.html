Inspiration

   Learning Web Development is hard; it's not that any single part is insurmountable - it is just that there are so many pieces - and they keep changing. It reminds me of when my family was in a jigsaw puzzle phase; sometimes there were multiple puzzles started. When we weren't looking, the dog, the cats and sometimes the ferrets would knock the pieces on the floor, mix them all up and even chew up a few, leaving us to sort through the mess.

   Many of the resources I've suggested either represent the latest trends or are comprehensive tutorial series that continually update their coursework. Think of these tools as the clean-up crew for all the missing and damaged puzzle pieces. There are many fantastic people and companies provideing top-notch help. As a result, despite the difficult path, I have yet to wake up and regret this Web Developer journey. Give it a try and you just might get hooked too. Full steam ahead!
